About Nexus Engineering & Planning

Innovating Urban Development for a Sustainable Future

Who We Are

Nexus Engineering & Planning Ltd. is a multidisciplinary consultancy firm dedicated to delivering transformative engineering and planning solutions. Our mission is to drive sustainable urban development and infrastructure across Africa.

With a team of experienced professionals, we combine advanced technology, innovative strategies, and a deep understanding of local contexts to deliver impactful projects.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To design and implement sustainable engineering and planning solutions that transform urban environments, empower communities, and foster economic growth.

Our Vision

To be the leading catalyst for resilient, inclusive, and innovative urban transformation in Africa and beyond.

Our Core Values

Innovation

We embrace cutting-edge technologies and creative thinking to solve complex challenges.

Sustainability

We prioritize environmentally responsible practices that ensure long-term viability and community well-being.

Integrity

We uphold the highest ethical standards in all our engagements, ensuring transparency and trustworthiness.

Meet Our Team

Olufemi Olamijulo

Founder

Olufemi Olamijulo is our Founder. With over a 7 years of experience in civil engineering, urban planning, and public policy, he leads multidisciplinary teams in delivering sustainable urban development solutions across Africa. With two graduate degrees from Harvard, his project experience spans several organizations including the United Nations Headquaters and the Lincoln Institute of Land Policy.

Engr. Benjamin Olamijulo

Co-Founder & Head of Projects

Engr. Benjamin Olamijulo holds a Bachelor of Science in Civil Engineering from the University of Lagos and is a Fellow of the Nigerian Society of Engineers. With nearly 30 years of experience, he has managed major projects in project design, supervision, and civil engineering construction. He is also a registered engineer with COREN.
