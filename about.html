About Nexus Engineering & Planning

Innovating Urban Development for a Sustainable Future

Who We Are

Nexus Engineering & Planning Ltd. is a multidisciplinary consultancy firm dedicated to delivering transformative engineering and planning solutions. Our mission is to drive sustainable urban development and infrastructure across Africa.

With a team of experienced professionals, we combine advanced technology, innovative strategies, and a deep understanding of local contexts to deliver impactful projects.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To design and implement sustainable engineering and planning solutions that transform urban environments, empower communities, and foster economic growth.

Our Vision

To be the leading catalyst for resilient, inclusive, and innovative urban transformation in Africa and beyond.

Meet Our Team

Femi Olamijulo

Founder

Femi Olamijulo is the Founder of Nexus. With over 7 years of experience spanning civil engineering, urban planning, and public policy, he leads multidisciplinary teams to deliver impactful development solutions. He holds two graduate degrees from Harvard and has contributed to initiatives at the United Nations and the Lincoln Institute of Land Policy.

Engr. Benjamin Olamijulo

Co-Founder & Head of Projects

Engr. Benjamin Olamijulo holds a Bachelor of Science in Civil Engineering from the University of Lagos and is a Fellow of the Nigerian Society of Engineers. With over 30 years of experience, he has managed major projects in project design, supervision, and civil engineering construction. He is also a registered engineer with COREN.

Tobi Ojenike

Corporate Communications

Tobi brings a strong background in communications, writing, and strategic engagement to Nexus Engineering. With a degree in International Relations from Covenant University and experience across corporate communications, media relations, and content strategy, Tobi helps craft messaging that strengthens our brand and builds industry relationships. Passionate about storytelling and community, Tobi also volunteers with the Lagos Food Bank and shares insights on leadership and well-being.
